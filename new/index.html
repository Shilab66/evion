FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Question

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our platform

What is Evion?

Evion is a centralized precision agriculture platform powered by a synthetic NDVI AI model that achieves over 98% accuracy in crop health and stress detection. We provide farmers with deep, scalable, and cost-effective insights—no expensive hardware or tedious processing required.

How does Evion work without near infrared or satellite imagery?

Evion uses a proprietary synthetic NDVI model trained on a diverse dataset of satellite, UAV, and field-level data. This allows us to simulate high-resolution, crop-specific NDVI visuals without the need of NIR.

What kind of data does Evion need?

All Evion needs is basic farm information such as simple RGB-camera drone captures (like the camera on your phone!). Our synthetic AI model handles the rest. No expensive/complicated drone modifications, satellite subscriptions, or expensive sensors needed.

What’s NDVI and why does it matter?

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) is a proven metric that reflects plant health, stress, and biomass. Farmers, agronomists, and researchers rely on NDVI to make data-driven decisions on irrigation, fertilization, pest control, and harvesting.

How accurate is Evion?

Our synthetic NDVI model has been tested across diverse crops and geographies and consistently delivers over 98% accuracy, outperforming conventional drone or satellite scans in accessibility, timeliness, and resolution.
